Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 lb. (90% lean) ground beef
  • 1 lb. ground pork
  • 3 tbsp. tomato paste
  • 2 (32-oz.) jars marinara sauce
  • 2 tsp. kosher salt

Steps

  1. In a large pot over medium-high heat, heat oil.
  2. Cook beef and pork, breaking up with a wooden spoon, until no longer pink, about 5 minutes.
  3. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add tomato paste to pot and cook over medium-high heat, stirring, until incorporated, about 1 minute.
  5. Add marinara sauce and stir to combine.
  6. Bring to a boil, then stir in 1 teaspoon salt.
  7. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally, until flavors have melded, about 20 minutes.
  8. Season with remaining 1 teaspoon salt, if needed.
